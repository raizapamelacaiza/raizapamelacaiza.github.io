Pamela Caiza-Guaman

Labor Markets · AI & Employment · Green Jobs · Universidad del Rosario.

Welcome! I am a researcher at the Regional Competitiveness Studies Center (SCORE) and the Labor Observatory (LaboUR) at Universidad del Rosario, Bogotá. My research focuses on labor markets, with particular interest in AI exposure, green jobs, energy transition, and gender inequality.

I hold an M.Sc. in Economics from Universidad del Rosario (Colombia) and a B.A. in Economics ad Finance from Escuela Politécnica Nacional (Ecuador). You can find my CV here.

If you would like to get in touch, please email me at raizapa.caiza@urosario.edu.co.
